# Signal Weights

## Agent Signal Weighting for Consensus

### Primary Signal Weights
- **Trend Following**: 30% - Primary directional indicator
- **Technical Analysis**: 25% - Chart patterns and indicators
- **Volume Analysis**: 20% - Liquidity and flow confirmation
- **Market Regime**: 15% - Current market conditions
- **Risk Assessment**: 10% - Risk-adjusted signal validation

### Signal Strength Multipliers
- **Strong Signal** (confidence > 0.8): 1.5x weight
- **Medium Signal** (confidence 0.6-0.8): 1.0x weight
- **Weak Signal** (confidence < 0.6): 0.5x weight

### Consensus Thresholds
- **Strong Consensus**: > 75% agreement
- **Moderate Consensus**: 60-75% agreement
- **Weak Consensus**: 40-60% agreement
- **No Consensus**: < 40% agreement

### Minimum Requirements
- Minimum 60% consensus for trade execution
- Minimum 3 agents must agree
- Risk assessment must be favorable
- Liquidity requirements must be met

## Weight Adjustment Factors

### Market Conditions
- **High Volatility**: Reduce trend weight, increase risk weight
- **Low Volume**: Increase liquidity weight importance
- **Strong Trend**: Increase trend following weight
- **Choppy Market**: Reduce all weights, increase risk threshold

### Time Frame Adjustments
- **Intraday**: Emphasize volume and microstructure
- **Swing Trading**: Balance trend and technical analysis
- **Position Trading**: Emphasize trend and regime analysis
